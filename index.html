ABOUT CRMD

The National level Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues Memorial Debate is a prestigious Debate Competition in Mumbai, hosted annually by Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering, Bandra. Inaugurated in the year 1999 as a debate competition for colleges within Mumbai, it became a National level Competition in 2012. This year, we celebrate the 25th year of this esteemed competition. It is a 2 day event held on 6th and 7th October, 2023 organised by our Students’ Council. The debate aims to provide a forum for today ’s youth to articulate views and engage in a dialogue on various issues.

Debating, a dance of unresolved thoughts, trumps the hasty conclusion.

  1. 1999 The Advent of CRMD
    Heralding the beginning of spirited debates and the development of persuasive speaking skills.
  2. 2012 India: An Order Rising Amongst Chaos
    India: An Order Rising Amongst Chaos - Delving into India's remarkable journey of establishing order and progress within the backdrop of complexity and chaos.
  3. 2013 India: Shifting towards Equinox
    Examining India's ongoing transformation towards a more balanced state in the midst of a rapidly changing global landscape.
  4. 2014 Entranced by Light, Entrapped by Shadows
    Captivated by the brilliance of light yet ensnared by the darkness of shadows, a complex exploration of contrasts and complexities.
  5. 2015 Appeasing mist shrouding the hills of reality
    Seeking solace in the soothing mist that veils the harsh contours of reality, a journey of comfort and escape.
  6. 2016 Breaking the Confines of Conformity
    Defying conformity to embrace individuality, these trailblazers inspire change and spark innovation
  7. 2017 Competence of Latent Judgement
    The capacity for nuanced and insightful decision-making, often hidden until circumstances require it.
  8. 2018 Hegemony of Moral Absolution
    The dominance of rigid and unyielding moral principles, often leading to intolerance of differing perspectives.
  9. 2019 Ideologies Egocentrism Engendering Self Abatement
    Ideological egocentrism fosters self-reduction as it prioritizes one's beliefs above all else.
  10. 2020 Immanent Critique of Social Adiaphora
    Immanent critique of social adiaphora delves deep into the subtle complexities that often go unnoticed in societal norms and values.
  11. 2021 The Emanation of Unbecoming Comportment
    The emanation of unbecoming comportment reflects a departure from accepted social norms and decorum.
  12. 2022 The Indian Agenda: Global Influence and Dominance
    The pursuit of global influence and dominance by India as a key player on the international stage.
  13. 2023 Sustainable Coalescence: A Socio-Economic Odyssey
    A compelling journey towards socio-economic sustainability through collaboration and unity.
